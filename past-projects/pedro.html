PEDRO

our first quadcopter

built in collaboration with my 2 amigos

Circa 2013, during our formative high school years; we endeavoured to dip our toes in the waters of aerial robotics. At the time the gear was very expensive and had to be purchased from overseas which took quite some time to arrive in our regional city. After months of researching, trial and error, tears and blood sacrifice... we had our maiden flight.
